## PAGE: How It Works

> ### 1. Design Your Card
> Pick your product and upload your photo(s), then customize every detail: choose your **finish** (holographic, foil, or matte), **border style**, the **name and text** on the card, the **style** (Pokémon or One Piece), and the **grade** on the slab label. Submit it all through our quick order form.
>
> ### 2. Approve Your Design
> Within **48 hours** we'll send a watermarked digital proof by **text or email** — your choice. Want changes? Just reply — we'll revise until you love it. Nothing prints until you approve.
>
> ### 3. We Print & Ship
> Once you approve, we print, slab, and ship within **48 hours**. You'll get a tracking number by email.
>
> **Typical total turnaround:** about 4–7 business days plus shipping time.

## PAGE: FAQ

> **Are these real graded cards?**
> No. These are **custom, fan-made novelty cards** in display slabs we designed to *look* like graded cards. They are not authentic PSA, BGS, or CGC graded cards, have no grading authority behind them, and the "grade" on the label is cosmetic and chosen by you.
>
> **Are you affiliated with Pokémon or One Piece?**
> No. We are an independent custom-design shop. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or licensed by Nintendo, The Pokémon Company, Bandai, or any rights holder. We don't reproduce official cards — we make original custom designs from your photos.
>
> **What photos can I send?**
> Clear, high-resolution photos you own the rights to (your own photos work best). Please don't send copyrighted or celebrity images you don't have permission to use.
>
> **How do I approve my design?**
> We send a proof by **text or email** (your choice at checkout), usually within 48 hours. You approve or request changes by reply — nothing prints until you say go.
>
> **Are the proofs watermarked?**
> Yes. Design proofs are sent as **watermarked, preview-quality images** so you can review them. Your finished card and slab are printed in full, clean, high resolution — the watermark only appears on the digital preview, never on the product you receive.
>
> **How long does it take?**
> About 4–7 business days to make + ship, plus delivery time. Need it faster? Add the **Rush** option at checkout.
>
> **Where do you ship?**
> From Canada, across Canada, to the US, and internationally. Rates are shown at checkout; free shipping over $50.
>
> **Can I get a refund?**
> Because every item is custom-made, we can't accept returns for change-of-mind, but we'll fix or replace anything damaged or wrong on our end. See our Refund Policy.
>
> **How do I reach you?**
> DM us on Instagram [@cardvaultcustoms](https://www.instagram.com/cardvaultcustoms) or email [your email here].
