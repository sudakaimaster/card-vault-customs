Our Story

Two people, a love of cards, and a lot of heart.

Card Vault Customs is a small family business turning your favourite moments into custom collector cards — sealed in premium display slabs, made one at a time.

How it started

We're a husband-and-wife team who grew up loving trading cards — the thrill of a rare pull, the art, the nostalgia. We also love giving gifts that actually mean something.

So we combined the two: custom Pokémon and One Piece-style cards made from your photos — your kids, your pets, your partner, your gamer alter-ego — and sealed in a slab that looks like the real graded deal.

What started as gifts for friends turned into something people kept asking us to make. So here we are.

What makes us different

Handmade by us

Every card is designed and assembled by the two of us — no factory, no outsourcing. Real attention on every order.

Designed with you

You approve a proof before anything prints. We tweak until it's exactly what you pictured — your vision, your call.

Premium & built to display

Crisp printing and a sturdy display slab made to be shown off on a shelf, a desk, or handed over as a gift to remember.

Our promise

We treat every order like it's a gift for someone we love — because to you, it usually is. If something isn't right, we'll make it right. That's the whole point of buying from real people.
