Refund & Return Policy

Custom-made items

Because every order is personalized and made to order, we do not accept returns or exchanges for change of mind.

Your approval protects you

We send a digital proof before printing — please review it carefully, as we print exactly what you approve.

Damaged or wrong items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or different from your approved proof, contact us within 7 days with photos at cardvaultcustoms@gmail.com and we'll repair, replace, or refund it.

Cancellations

You can cancel for a full refund any time before you approve your design proof. Once approved and in production, orders can't be cancelled.

Shipping Policy

We ship from Canada.

  • Processing: Orders are designed and proofed within ~48 hours; once you approve, we ship within ~48 hours. Typical total: 4–7 business days before transit.
  • Rates: Flat $15.99 CAD shipping — free on orders over $75 CAD. All prices are in Canadian dollars.
  • Destinations: Canada, United States, and international.
  • Tracking: A tracking number is emailed when your order ships.
  • Customs & duties: International customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes.
  • Lost or delayed: We're not responsible for carrier delays, but we'll help you track and resolve any issues.

Privacy Policy

Card Vault Customs collects the information you provide (name, shipping address, email, phone number, and uploaded images) only to fulfill your order and contact you about it. Payments are processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card details.

We use your phone number solely to send your design proof and order updates. We do not sell your personal information. Uploaded images are used only to create your order and are not shared publicly without your permission (we'll always ask before featuring your card on our social media).

Uploaded photos are automatically deleted 30 days after your order is delivered. We keep them during that window only so we can quickly repair or reprint your order if anything goes wrong.

To request earlier deletion of your photos or any other data, email cardvaultcustoms@gmail.com.
