## ⚠️ THE MOST IMPORTANT PART: Custom Novelty / IP Disclaimer

Selling cards that mimic Pokémon/One Piece designs and a "graded slab" look carries **intellectual-property and consumer-protection risk**. To stay on the right side of it:

1. **Only print custom, original designs** — never reproduce or sell copies of real Pokémon/One Piece cards.
2. **Never imply official licensing or real grading.** Avoid the actual PSA/BGS/CGC logos and trademarks on your slabs and labels.
3. **Show this disclaimer** on every product page, in the FAQ, and in the footer.
4. **Use customer-owned photos.** Don't let customers upload copyrighted/celebrity images they don't own — state this in your terms.

**Disclaimer text to display:**
> Card Vault Customs creates original, custom, fan-made novelty cards and display slabs. Our products are **not** authentic graded cards and are **not** affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or licensed by Nintendo, The Pokémon Company, Game Freak, Creatures Inc., Bandai, Toei Animation, or any other rights holder. Any "grade" shown is a cosmetic, customer-selected design element with no grading authority behind it. All character names and trademarks are the property of their respective owners; we do not reproduce official cards. By ordering, you confirm you have the right to use any image you submit.
